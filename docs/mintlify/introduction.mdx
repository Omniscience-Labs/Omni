## What is Omni?

Omni is an enterprise AI agent platform that enables your team to build, deploy, and manage autonomous AI agents that handle complex workflows. From research and data analysis to content creation and automation, Omni agents work alongside your team to boost productivity.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In with SSO">
    Access Omni through your organization's single sign-on:
    - Click **Continue with Microsoft** for enterprise deployments
    - Or use **Continue with Google** for Google Workspace
    - Complete two-factor authentication if required
    - First-time users receive a guided tour of the interface
    
    <Tip>
      Your admin will send an invitation email with access instructions. Omni uses your existing enterprise credentials - no separate password needed.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your Agent">
    Start with a pre-built agent from the marketplace or create your own custom agent with specific capabilities and knowledge.
